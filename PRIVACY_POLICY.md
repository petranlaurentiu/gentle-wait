# GentleWait Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

## Overview

GentleWait helps you pause before opening selected apps. We designed the app to keep as much data on your device as possible.

## Data We Store On Device

GentleWait stores the following on your device:

- Protected app selections
- Pause settings and onboarding preferences
- Pause history and reflection events
- Journal entries you write inside the app
- Exercise and ritual preferences

This information is used to power the app experience and insights inside GentleWait.

## Android Accessibility Service

On Android, GentleWait uses an Accessibility Service to detect when you open an app you chose to protect. This allows GentleWait to bring up its pause screen before you continue.

GentleWait does not use Accessibility Service to read the text you type, the messages you send, or the content you view inside other apps.

## Purchases And Subscriptions

If you use premium features, GentleWait uses RevenueCat and the underlying app store billing systems to manage subscriptions, purchases, and entitlement status.

## AI Companion

If the AI Companion is enabled in your build and you choose to use it, your messages and relevant in-app context are sent to GentleWait's backend and then to its AI provider in order to generate a response.

AI requests are only made when you actively use the AI Companion feature.

## Data Sharing

GentleWait does not sell your personal data.

We only share data with service providers that are necessary to operate optional paid or AI features, such as:

- RevenueCat for subscription management
- App store billing providers
- AI infrastructure providers for AI Companion requests

## Your Choices

You can:

- Disable Android accessibility access at any time in system settings
- Remove protected apps from GentleWait at any time
- Clear app data through your device settings
- Stop using premium or AI features at any time
